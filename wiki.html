Installation Guide

Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to get started with MeowCraft:

  1. Download the Launcher: Head to our download page and get the MeowCraft launcher for your operating system.
  2. Install: Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  3. Login/Register: Create an account or login with your existing credentials.

Gameplay

Engage in competitive battles with other players!

Battle Commands

  • /battle [player] - Challenge another player
  • /battle accept - Accept a battle request
  • /battle decline - Decline a battle request
  • /battle format [format] - Choose battle format

Battle Formats

  • Singles: 1v1 battles
  • Doubles: 2v2 battles
  • Triples: 3v3 battles
  • Custom: Set your own rules

Land Claiming

Gameplay

Protect your builds and items with our comprehensive claim system.

Basic Commands

  • /claim - Create a claim (requires Golden Shovel)
  • /trust [player] - Give a player full access to your claim
  • /untrust [player] - Remove trust from a player
  • /abandonclaim - Delete the claim you're standing in
  • /claimlist - List all your claims
üí° Pro Tip: Use /subdivide to create smaller areas within your main claim for specific players!
