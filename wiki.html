Installation Guide

Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to get started with MeowCraft:

  1. Download the Launcher: Head to our download page and get the MeowCraft launcher.
  2. Install: Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  3. Login/Register: Create an account or login with your existing credentials.
  4. Launch: Click "Play" and the launcher will automatically download all required mods.
Tip: Make sure you have Java 17 or higher installed for optimal performance.

First Steps

Getting Started

Once you've joined the server, here's what to do:

1. Choose Your Starter

When you first join, you'll be able to choose from a selection of starter Pokémon.

2. Claim Your Land

Protect your builds using our land claim system. Use /claim to secure your area.

3. Explore the World

Our custom-generated world has unique biomes and structures waiting to be discovered.

4. Join the Community

Connect with other players on our Discord server and join in on events!

PvP Battles

Gameplay

Engage in competitive battles with other players!

Battle Commands

  • /battle [player] - Challenge another player
  • /battle accept - Accept a battle request
  • /battle decline - Decline a battle request

Land Claiming

Gameplay

Protect your builds and items with our claim system.

Basic Commands

  • /claim - Create a claim
  • /trust [player] - Give access to your claim
  • /untrust [player] - Remove access

Economy System

Economy

Learn how to earn and spend money in MeowCraft!

Economy Commands

  • /balance - Check your balance

  • /shop - Access the shop
