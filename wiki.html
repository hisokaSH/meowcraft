Your complete guide to mastering MeowCraft

Getting Started

New to MeowCraft? Here's everything you need to know to begin your adventure.

  • Download the MeowCraft Launcher
  • Create an account or login
  • Click Play and join the server
  • Choose your starter Pokémon
Tip: Make sure you have Java 17+ installed!

Essential Commands

Master these commands to navigate the server like a pro.

  • /spawn - Return to spawn
  • /home - Teleport home
  • /sethome - Set your home
  • /tpa [player] - Request teleport
  • /balance - Check money
  • /pay [player] [amount] - Send money

Land Claiming

Protect your builds and items from other players.

  • /claim - Create a claim
  • /trust [player] - Add trusted player
  • /untrust [player] - Remove trust
  • /abandonclaim - Delete claim
  • /claimlist - View your claims

Cobblemon Basics

Learn how to catch, train, and battle with your Pokémon.

  • Throw Pokéballs at wild Pokémon
  • Battle to gain experience
  • Level up to evolve
  • Heal at Pokémon Centers
  • Trade with other players

Economy System

Earn PokéCoins and spend them wisely!

  • Daily login rewards
  • Sell items at /shop
  • Auction house /ah
  • Complete quests for rewards
  • Win events and tournaments

PvP Battles

Challenge other trainers to prove your skills!

  • /battle [player] - Challenge
  • /battle accept - Accept battle
  • /battle decline - Decline
Battle formats: Singles, Doubles, Triples

Server Rules

Follow these rules to keep MeowCraft fun for everyone.

  • Be respectful to all players
  • No griefing or stealing
  • No cheating or exploiting
  • Keep chat family-friendly
  • No advertising other servers
Breaking rules may result in bans!

FAQ

How do I get started?

Download our launcher from the Download page, create an account, and click Play!

Is the server always online?

Yes! MeowCraft runs 24/7 with 99.9% uptime.

Can I play with friends?

Absolutely! Invite friends to join and explore together.

How do I report bugs?

Join our Discord and use the #bug-reports channel.

What Minecraft version?

MeowCraft runs on Fabric 1.21.1 with Cobblemon.

How do I get more Pokémon?

Explore different biomes! Each has unique spawns.
